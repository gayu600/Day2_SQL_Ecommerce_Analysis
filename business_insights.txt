Day 2 - E-Commerce Sales Database Analysis

1. Monthly Revenue Insight
The monthly revenue trend shows how sales revenue changed across months. This helps identify high-performing and low-performing months.

2. Order Status Insight
Most orders are completed/delivered, which indicates successful order processing. Cancelled or unavailable orders can affect customer satisfaction.

3. Top Products Insight
A small number of products contribute high revenue. These products can be prioritized for promotions and stock management.

4. Customer Insight
Top customers contribute more orders/revenue. These customers can be targeted with loyalty offers.
